### Tolerances
to find the resistance boundaries use the formula $R\pm T \cdot R$ where;
$T$ is the tolerance,
$R$ is the resistance.

## Ohm's Law
---
Ohm's law is a relation between the voltage ($V$), current ($I$) and resistance ($R$)
$V=I\cdot R$
$I=\dfrac{V}{R}$
$R=\dfrac{V}{I}$
